Welcome to the AGGORA Technical asset management app – a handy way to manage your timesheet on the go!


The app aims to minimize the time spent by engineers for doing their timesheets. Checkin at the beginnning of each day and checkout at the end of each working day. Timesheet automatically retreives all the job and travel related information with a simple push of button. It is ever so easy to add the time spent on other activities during the working hours such as lunch, parts collection, etc by simply dragging the time slots on the calendar.
